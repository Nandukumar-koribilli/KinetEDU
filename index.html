About KinetEdu

KinetEdu is a centralized, digital operating system built for secondary, polytechnic, and engineering students in India. We aim to replace disorganized study groups and fragmented resource repositories with a single, streamlined, and high-performance portal.

Our platform empowers students with structured roadmaps, interactive problem-solving tools, and community-driven rewards. By combining student feedback with state-of-the-art technological features, KinetEdu makes studying less stressful and more rewarding.
